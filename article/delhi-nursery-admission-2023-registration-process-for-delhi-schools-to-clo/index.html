Delhi Nursery Admission 2023: The registration process for admission to nursery classes in Delhi schools will end on December 23 after starting on December 1. The registration process is underway for admission to open seats in Pre-School, Pre-Primary and Class 1 in private unaided recognized schools. of Delhi for the session 2023-24. Parents can apply till today at edudel.nic.in. Delhi Nursery Admission 2023: Important Dates Admission Process – December 1, 2022 Last date for registration – December 23, 2022 The first list of selected students – January 20, 2023 Solution of parents’ questions against the first list – January 21 to January 30, 2023 Second list of selected students – February 6, 2023 Resolution of queries of parents against the second list – February 8 to 14, 2023 Also read:- JEE Advanced 2023: Exam schedule releases at jeeadv.ac.in, check complete process here Age limit Parents may note that the maximum age of their child to fill the application form by 31st March 2023 is at least four years for admission in Nursery, five years for KG (Pre-Primary) and at least six for Class 1 Must be the year. The second list to release on this day The first list of selected students will be released on January 20 next year and the second list to release on February 6. If parents have any objections regarding the first and second list, they can apply for the resolution process from January 21 to January 30 and February 8 to 14 respectively. The admission process will close on March 17 next year. Details of all entry-level classes along with seats that will be available for admission in the following year will be announced by all schools by December 16, 2022. Parents will be able to upload the details of their children to take admission under open seats in January. 6. The marks awarded to each will be uploaded on 13th January 2023 as per a scoring system. Parents are advised to keep checking the DOE website regularly for the latest updates. How to apply for Delhi Nursery Admission? Parents can get the application form for admission by visiting the school nearest to their present place of residence (permanent or temporary) for admission of their child to the nursery class. Submit this form by filling it out completely and visiting the concerned school along with the necessary documents (Aadhar card of parents, residence proof, child’s birth certificate, child’s photo, family photo, etc.). However, many schools are accepting applications for nursery admission online mode on their website.
