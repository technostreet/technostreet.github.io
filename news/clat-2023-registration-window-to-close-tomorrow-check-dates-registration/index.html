CLAT 2023: The Consortium of National Law Universities(NLUs) is to close the registration window for CLAT 2023 tomorrow, November 18, 2022. Candidates who have not registered themselves yet can do the same by visiting the official website of consortium — consortiumofnlus.ac.in — Candidates are advised to submit their application form before the deadline. Earlier, the last date to register for the same was November 13, which was extended by the consortium to November 18, 2022. Applicants are advised not to miss the deadline. Read the official notice here CLAT 2023: Important dates Application form last date — November 18, 2022 Admit card — Last week of November 2022 Exam date– December 18, 2022 How to fill out the application form? Visit the official website — consortiumofnlus.ac.in — Click on CLAT 2022 link and register as a new user Fill out the application form by entering personal, academic details, and communication details along with NLU preferences Submit and complete the process by paying the registration fee CLAT 2023: Registration fee For candidates in the general category, the CLAT 2023 application cost is Rs. 4,000. While the registration cost for those who are SC/ST or BPL is Rs. 3,500. Candidates can pay the application cost online using a method like net banking, a credit card, or a debit card. Direct link to register For entrance to the 22 National Law Universities (NLUs) and numerous more private institutions, the CLAT exam is held annually. On December 18, 2022, several test centres around the nation will host the exam in an offline format. On their admit cards, candidates can view the exam centres that have been assigned to them.
