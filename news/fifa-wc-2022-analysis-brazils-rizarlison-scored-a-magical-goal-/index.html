Brazil registered their first win in Group G of the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar with a 2-0 win over Serbia. Brazil player Rizalison scored 2 goals. His second goal was the pinnacle of wonder. Magical goal is what this goal is. It has been described as the best goal of this World Cup. Serbia played very bravely from the start of the game. Although Brazil managed to penetrate the Serbian goal circle several times in the first half, Serbian goal keeper Vanja Milinkovic put severe obstacles to Brazil’s striking power. Brazil had more of the ball in the first half. In the 27th minute of the match, Brazil’s Thiago Silva brilliantly parried goalkeeper Milinkovic to shoot a low shot towards goal. Milinkovic also blocked a shot towards the Rabinia goal. After the break, Brazil used all their energy and played terribly. This is artistic football. Again Rabinya’s effort from the wing was blocked by the Serbian goalkeeper. Then Brazil’s Vinicius Junior’s precision shot from distance hit the goal post and the goalpost swung all over, a terrific shot but no goal. Brazil eventually took a 1-0 lead in the 62nd minute when the Serbian goalkeeper saved a shot from Brazilian Vinicius Jr. Magical Goal – The best goal of this World Cup: In the 73rd minute of the match, Vinicius cut the ball in from outside the box on the left side to take the ball to two Brazilian players one-on-one on the left side of the field. Rizalison, who was standing there as if controlling the ball, stopped the ball slightly in the air with his left leg, spun around and took a kick with his right leg… A somewhat unexpected move, the ball flew into the goal. Hit the net. This is the best goal of this World Cup. Usually such shots are played in training, or played in leagues, in serious World Cup matches it is often not played in the goal mouth as it is a risk. But this magical shot gives us an idea of ​​what the Risarlison training must have been like. Stopped with his left foot, turned while the ball was still in the air, bent his body and shot it into the goal with his right foot. It is still an unbelievable goal. Brazil lead 2-0. Rizal Son has scored 9 goals for Brazil this year with this wonder goal. Neymar Injury – Serbia’s Tide Game: Instead of handling Brazilian star Neymar properly, Serbia committed more fouls and handled Neymar the way other teams do. 9 of Serbia’s 12 fouls were against Neymar. In the end, as usual, Neymar left with tears. Serbia can’t be blamed, their strategy often stifled Brazil, with goalkeeper Vanja Milinkovic making a brilliant save. As a result, Brazil had to make some long-range shot attempts. Because no matter how you hit left, right, slightly higher, the Serbian goalkeeper stops it. Serbian coach Dragan Stojkovic crowded the midfield in the first half. The prevention strategy is not set so closely. With this strategy of his, he stopped the threat of Brazil playing as a team and played a fierce game of individuals. However, the Serbian forwards lacked speed. This made it impossible to launch a counter attack. The forwards Dusan Tadic, Aleksandar Mitrovic and Sergej Milinkovic were slow and could not move the ball quickly. Brazil’s Rizalison could have scored 2 goals. But another player who played brilliantly was Vinicius. There’s no denying that his pace down the side of the field created many chances for Brazil in the first half. Rizarlison is playing in his first World Cup series. Rizarlison became the first player to score the first 2 goals at the World Cup after Kareka (1990), Neymar (2014) and Philippe Courtinho (2018).
