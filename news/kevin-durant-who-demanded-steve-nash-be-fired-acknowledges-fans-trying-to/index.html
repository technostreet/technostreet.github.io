Ja Morant, Desmond Bane, and co. kept the Nets’ ‘All-Star duo of Kevin Durant and Kyrie Irving at bay, en route to a magnificent victory. The Nets pair however, were phenomenal in their losing battle. Both men put up 35+ points (37 each) on an efficient night, yet they wound up being defeated. Perhaps the duo needs their teammates to provide some assistance, rather than burden the majority of the weight on their shoulders.
