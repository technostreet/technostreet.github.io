The Indian cricket team has wished wicket-keeper batsman Rishabh Pant a speedy recovery, as they send a video for the 25-year-old. Other players in the team, including coach Rahul Dravid and captain Hardik Pandya, have sent messages to Pant ahead of the 3-match T20 series against Sri Lanka. BCCI shared a video on social media on Tuesday, January 3. In this, the head coach and team members called Rishabh Pant as a fighter and asked him to get well soon. For the unversed, Rishabh Pant had a massive accident in Roorkee, Uttarakhand on Friday, December 30. His car collided with the dividers at the Dehradun-Delhi Highway. After this Pant is admitted to Max hospital in Dehradun and has sustained a fracture in his knee. There are stitches on his forehead and there is also an injury in his hand and back. After this accident, the Indian wicket keeper batsman will have to stay away from professional cricket for a long time. It is believed that Pant will take at least six months to recover completely and get back to the ground. Team India sends a special message The video begins with Head coach Rahul Dravid hoping Rishabh is on his way to recovery. He then added that he had the opportunity to work with Pant in the last one year. During this, the 25-year-old has played many memorable innings in Indian Test history, that too in difficult conditions. “I know you know how to get out of such situations. It’s a challenge and you will bounce back, you’ve done this before. Hope to see you soon,” added Rahul Dravid. Later Hardik Pandya, who is currently leading Team India in IND vs SL T20 Series featured in the video. He firstly wished Rishabh a speedy recovery. And said that Pant is a fighter and things are not always the same. “You will break every door and make a comeback. Whole team and the country is with you,” said Pandya. What did Suryakumar Yadav, Yuzvendra Chahal and Ishan Kishan said? Suryakumar Yadav said, “I am praying for your speedy recovery. I know how things are now. We are missing you here. Praying for your return as soon as possible. You are a fighter.” At the same time, Yuzvendra Chahal also wished Pant a speedy recovery. He asked the Indian wicket keeper-batsman to get well soon and come back. “Let’s hit fours and sixes together soon,” said Yuzi Chahal. Later Ishan Kishan featured in the video and said that they are all missing Pant in the Indian team. Ishan also wished him a speedy recovery too. “I know you are a fighter and will come back stronger than before,” said Kishan. Shubman Gill also added, I am wishing you a speedy recovery on behalf of the entire team. We all know the kind of person you are.
